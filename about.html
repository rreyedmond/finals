Che-che RC Marketing owned and managed by Lourdes Sinangote. She started her business peddling her homemade tablia in 1992. She named her product Che-che’s Tablia after her daughter’s name. After 13 years, she officially established her business with the help of her husband. They hired 15 people for production. They added corn coffee and salabat in their business. Were able to deliver for their regular customers and supply to the supermarkets in the province. After 10 years, they added cooking oil, eggs, spices, fruits and vegetables to their products. They sell their products in markets of every towns of the province and even in Cebu. In 2015, their daughter started in handling their business together with her husband. This year they named their business “Che-Che RC Marketing”.
